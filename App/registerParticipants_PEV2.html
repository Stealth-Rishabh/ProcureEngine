    Help

    Using this screen admin can register vendors and their representatives for the several type of bid like Air shipments, Domestic shipments, Sea shipments, Product & Services and Warehouse rentals.

    The key details that should be entered are: company name, Address, city, pan no., GST No, company phone no., company E-mail. Once all mandatory details are entered click on submit button. This will add vendor as a registered vendor in sourcing portal database and will send user id and password at companies email address to access sourcing portal.

    Company can be deactivated (if required) and their information can be edited by searching company name and clicking on edit button in grid.
