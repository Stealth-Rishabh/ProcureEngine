Help

Using this screen admin can register portal users and provide them access for configuring bids like Air shipments, Domestic shipments, Sea shipments, Product & Services and Warehouse rentals.

The register user screen should be used to register employee who will play role of portal user with access to their respective area only. It’s highly recommended to have only one administrator to avoid configuration issues. Users can be deactivated (if required) and their information can be edited by clicking edit button in grid.
