      Vendor action on VQ

      Once vendor will enter User Id password vendor will see following details:

      • Pending VQ/RFQ : vendor will see Pending VQ/RFQ for and click on it.
      • VQ Details : Vendor need to read Terms and conditions and Accept terms and conditions by clicking on it and select Next.
      • Company Information : Vendor Fill all company details click on save and continue button
      • Financial Information : Vendor need to fill financial details about the company
          need to select Yes on "Is company publicly listed
          Need to attach balance sheet of last two years
          Mention company revenue history
      • Finance sub category1 : Vendor must need to add the supporting documents if the field is mandatory and click on Save and continue"
      • Company capability : Need to mention the operational location of the company and add supporting documents ( if mandatory) and click on "Save and Continue"
      • Technical Information : Need to mention the technical details of the company and add supporting documents ( if mandatory) and click on "Save and Exit
      • Once vendor will click on "Save and Exit" an automated VQ response email will be generated against mentioned subject.
