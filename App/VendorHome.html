    Help

    This is the main landing page of our sourcing portal for vendors, which has direct access to frequently used functionalities as mentioned below

    Pending bid: This block shows all the bids along with date pending for participation. The vendor representative can click on individual bid and enter password received for the selected bid in email to participate.

    Pending RFI/RFQ: This block shows all the RFI/RFQ pending at company end. This also shows the deadline date of RFI/RFQ before which vendor should provide their responses. The vendor representative can click on individual RFI/RFQ to provide their responses.
