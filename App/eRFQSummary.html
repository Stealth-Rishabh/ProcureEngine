eRFQ Summary
Help

The purpose of RFQ summary report is to see the details of various rfq configured and participated using our sourcing portal. The report can be taken out using one or many filtration parameters. The filter parameters are; RFQ Subject, RFQ Status, From Date,Configured By,Report Type From and To Date.

Click on submit button after specifying filter criteria.
