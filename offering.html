Offerings

e-Negotiation Platform

From the evaluation of new and existing vendors to taking Techno-commercial bids to online negotiations using RFQ mode and e-Auction mode for both buying and selling, ProcurEngine simplifies the complex negotiation process in a highly secure environment. It is followed by a workflow-based approval to record the entire approval process.

e-Negotiation Training

ProcurEngine with its experience sharing modules from a variety of situations - spread across different industries - help negotiators to cross learn and prepare themselves for the newer challenges in the upcoming years. The training conducted in a workshop format aims to familiarize the Supply Chain team members with the modern tools available for negotiations and how various organizations are benefitting from that. It creates a perspective for the next 3-5 years on how technology is shaping negotiations and how critical it is for the organizations to get ready for that. Also, how Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning can be combined to hone the negotiation skills of our SCM and other relevant team members.

Negotiation Support

If you have a lean team or want to outsource the e-Auction process, procurEngine can take care of that. You can select the handing over point and procurEngine will do the rest. Clients can take strategic decisions based on the comparative techno-commercial statements thus generated as an output of the above process - thereby saving a lot of time and resources.

Purchase Portfolio Analysis

Purchase portfolio analysis shifts purchasers’ focus from looking at Purchase Requisitions from just doing Pareto Analysis (or ABC analysis) to studying trends and creating product category wise negotiation process to follow. Negotiation is an art and it gets perfected with experience and time. The important part is how to start. At procurEngine we follow advanced Purchase Portfolio Analysis models to slice and dice the procurement with shifting timeframes. The output thus generated helps to formulate start points for negotiation strategy for different products and services.

Consultancy Services

When the objective is to shift from 1st gear to overdrive, procurEngine consultancy services, with experts having wide experience of negotiations in a variety of situations, studies each case minutely and suggests the negotiation process to follow to derive maximum benefits. This helps clients to formulate a strategy for each case – thereby creating a long-term sustainable process.

Vendor Discovery

In case you are looking for new vendors for specific product or service groups, procurEngine can provide you a list of good vendors in these categories for better participation in enquiries and e-Auctions. ProcurEngine based on the product or service specifications, geography and client’s operational area offers vendor discovery process. The vendors are pre-qualified by ProcurEngine on the basis of its specific criteria. However, the client can run their own qualification process to approve them.
