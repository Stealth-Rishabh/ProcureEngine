Reverse/Forward Auction Process

The process starts from Bid Configuration, Automatic email invite to participant with unique password for every bid and how to participate manual, bid participation by vendors, automatic extension of bid time if best prices are quoted at last moment to provide fair opportunity to other participants, workflow based approvals once bid is over & participation summary reports. The process is designed to take care of following key areas.

RFQ process

The process start from RFQ invite which has provision to invite quotations at BOM/BOQ level, Response from vendors and summary reports based on received responses. This process is ideal in scenarios where reverse auction is not feasible due to long list of BOM/BOQ items.
